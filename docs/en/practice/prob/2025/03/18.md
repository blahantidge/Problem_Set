You are sitting South. Partner leads the ♦10. The ♦J is played from dummy. You win this trick. Declarer follows with a low diamond. You return ♦8. Declarer follows with another low diamond, and partner ruffs with the ♠2. Following your suit preference signal, partner leads the ♥3. A low heart is played from dummy, you play the ♥K, and declarer wins the ♥A. Declarer leads a spade. Partner follows with the ♠8, and the ♠Q is played from dummy. You naturally win the ♠A. Find a way to defeat the contract.
